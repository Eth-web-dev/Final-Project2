Our Little Story

Ethiopian Restaurant was started with one goal in mind. A neighborhood restaurant that brings traditional Ethiopian home cooking to a warm and inviting environment, bring the amazing and unique Ethiopian flavors to Raleigh, Cary, Durham, Morrisville, Chapel Hill, Wake Forest, and surrounding areas. We believe we have done exactly that.
We provide amazing quality by using only the best ingredients and by perfecting our techniques and recipes over and over again. We love our food. We hope that you love our food. Let us make some for you.
